Government Agency Website UI Redesign

The redesign of the government agency's website is a step towards improving user experience and accessibility, and the agency should continue to explore new and innovative ways to enhance its services and support users.

Problem Statement

The government agency's website was outdated and difficult to navigate, resulting in low user engagement and satisfaction.

Solution

Redesign the website, incorporating user-centered design principles and adhering to the latest design standards and accessibility guidelines. The goal was to create a user-friendly and accessible website that streamlined the user journey and enhanced user satisfaction and engagement.

My Role

I was responsible for the design of the website's user interface, ensuring that the design adhered to the latest design standards and accessibility guidelines. I worked independently, using my design skills to create a visually appealing and user-friendly website.
